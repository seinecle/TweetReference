TweetReference






I am applying for a position for which I need to provide references.

Why not ask for references on Twitter? After all, many Twitter users know more about aspects of my work than colleagues I see everyday in real life.

This web app provides the way to give and receive references from Twitter users. In the format of 140 characters max of course!

Use it and send me feedback at @seinecle. Oh, and help me get this job, write a nice reference for me!
